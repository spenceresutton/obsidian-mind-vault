## Progress

Northside attended a focused anticoagulation (heparin) demo on May 11. The meeting surfaced significant workflow pain around heparin dosing — currently paper flow sheets in Cerner. The team was introduced to both MIPD (anticoag module) and Navigator (ASP module).
